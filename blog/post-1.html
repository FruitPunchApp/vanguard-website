Vanguard Wheel Co.:
Built for the Road Ahead

VWC — Journal — 001

A wheel is a simple thing. Round, solid, meant to turn. But when it's made with care, it carries more than the weight of a car. It carries the thought that went into it, the hands that shaped it, and the driver who depends on it.

At Vanguard Wheel Co., we start with memory. Shapes from the past linger in the minds of people who love cars — the Porsche Cup, the Sprintstar, the BRM. They weren't just designs. They were a moment in time, a piece of culture, a way of seeing a car as something personal. We don't copy them. We listen. The lines, the spirit, the balance. Then we bend the old into something that belongs in the present. Stronger. Sharper. Ready for today.

The work is deliberate. Aluminum is forged, cut, measured for the fitment a customer wants. Every order is different. No two drivers want the same wheel in the same way. Some want a wide stance for the track. Some want a narrow fit for a vintage car. Some want the look of the past but the strength of today. Vanguard makes space for all of it.

When the boxes arrive at the customer's door, the work is quiet. The old wheels come off. The new ones go on. Lug nuts tighten. The car lowers to the ground. In that small act, everything changes. The car feels more complete. More like it belongs to its driver.

That's what Vanguard is about. Not just metal. Not just design. But something that feels yours — and yours alone. Something that honors history while still moving forward.

The road is out there. Vanguard wheels are ready for it.
