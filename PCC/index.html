The Design

A Modern Reinterpretation
of a Porsche Classic

The Vanguard PCC is inspired by the iconic Porsche "Cookie Cutter" wheels of the 1970s and 1980s, celebrated for their sculpted slots and motorsport aesthetic.

This modern reinterpretation maintains the bold graphic character while incorporating forged monoblock construction for lightweight strength and contemporary diameters.

Design Language

Familiar at First Glance.
Unmistakably Vanguard.

The Cookie Cutter's appeal was always in its graphic confidence — those deep, defined slots that caught light and shadow in equal measure. It was a wheel that looked fast standing still.

The PCC preserves that visual language without borrowing it wholesale. The proportions are recalibrated for modern sizing. The edges are resolved with more precision. The result honors the source while standing on its own.

Some designs fade with time. Others define it.
