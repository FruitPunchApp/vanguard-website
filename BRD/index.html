The Design

A Modern Tribute to
an Air-Cooled Icon

The Vanguard BRD draws direct inspiration from the unmistakable Volkswagen BRM wheel of the 1960s and '70s. Originally defined by its bold, turbine-like spokes and motorsport roots, the BRM became a symbol of performance within the air-cooled community.

The BRD carries that legacy forward with a refined, modern execution. Every surface has been carefully reinterpreted to maintain the spirit of the original while fitting it to today's vehicles.

Design Inspiration

Built on a Foundation
Worth Keeping

The original BRM wheel was never subtle. Its bold spoke pattern created motion even at a standstill, giving lightweight Volkswagens a strong, competition-driven look.

The BRD builds on that foundation. Edges are sharpened. Depth is enhanced. The result is a wheel that feels familiar at first glance, yet distinctly contemporary upon closer inspection.

This is not a reproduction. It's a progression.
Engineering

Engineered for
Modern Fitment

While rooted in vintage design, the BRD is built for modern performance and compatibility. Available in a range of diameters and widths, designed to properly fit today's performance-oriented builds while maintaining the visual balance that made the original so effective.

Lightweight construction and optimized spoke geometry ensure that form and function remain equally prioritized.

Ideal Applications
  • Air-cooled Volkswagen builds seeking a refined evolution of period-correct styling
  • Water-cooled VW platforms looking to incorporate vintage motorsport influence
  • Porsche applications where classic design language meets modern performance
  • Custom builds that demand a distinctive, directional wheel design
