About Vanguard Wheel Co.

We don't make
catalog wheels.

You can spend years getting a build exactly right: the stance, the bodywork, the mechanicals, and then put something completely forgettable on the corners. It happens all the time. But it shouldn't.

The wheels are what tie everything together. They MAKE the car. They're the first thing people notice and the last thing most builders think about. Vanguard Wheel Co. exists to close that gap.

Modern Form. Vintage Soul.

The designs draw from an era when wheels were a part of the design. When the spoke count, the dish, the lip all meant something. That language still holds up. What didn't hold up were the manufacturing methods. Modern manufacturing is lighter, stronger, and more precise than anything from the golden era. We use it without apology.
